Knabberfuchs

An honest calorie tracker.

Scan a barcode or search, type the grams, done. No ads, no subscription, no popups, no account. Works offline, and your data never leaves your phone.

Ad-free No subscription No popups Works offline Your data stays local

What it does

📷

Scan barcodes

Packaged products from Open Food Facts; whole foods from a bundled USDA dataset.

🔎

Fast local search

Hundreds of thousands of foods, with smart synonyms and ranking — no waiting.

🌍

Offline, worldwide

Download any of 106 country databases and scan + search with no connection.

🍽️

Track your way

Auto-grouped meals as you add, or classic Breakfast / Lunch / Dinner / Snacks.

📖

Recipes & sharing

Build recipes, log a portion to any day, and share by QR — no server involved.

🖼️

Meal from a photo

Snap a recipe's ingredient list; on-device OCR turns it into a meal to match.

➕

Add missing products

Barcode not found? Add it (OCR the label), and optionally contribute it to OFF.

❤️

Health Connect

Optionally sync your logged calories & macros to Android Health Connect.

💾

Your data, a zip

Export everything to a backup zip (JSON + CSV) and restore it any time.

Built on Open Food Facts ❤️

Knabberfuchs is built on Open Food Facts — a free, open, crowdsourced food database kept alive by volunteers around the world. Without their work, this app wouldn't exist.
